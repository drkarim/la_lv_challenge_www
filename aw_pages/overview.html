Challenge

The challenge is part of the Statistical Modelling and Computational Modeling of the Heart (STACOM) 2016 workshop to be held at MICCAI. STACOM is supported by the cardiac atlas project. MICCAI 2016 will be held from 17th to 21st October 2016 in Athens, Greece, sponsored by IBM, Medtronic, Siemens and Kitware

Datasets

The image included in the challenge consist of MRI (n=10) and CT (n=10) datasets.

Organisers

Five institutions took part in the organized challenge. Two of these institutions were commerical software providers. The participants were: 1) Department of Imaging Sciences and Biomedical Engineering at King's college London in United Kingdom. 2) Center for Computational Imaging and Simulation Technologies in Biomedicine at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona, Spain. 3) Alma IT systems in Barcelona, Spain. 4) Center for intelligence machine at McGill University in Canada. 5) Fraunhofer Institute for Medical Image Computing, Fraunhofer MEVIS in Germany.
