Sponsors

We wish to thank our sponsor National Institute for Health Research (NIHR) Biomedical Research Centre (BRC).

King's College London is one of the 11 NIHR BRC centres in the UK. These centres have a strong focus on translational research, taking advances in basic medical research out of the laboratory and into the clinical setting so that they can benefit patients.

We would also like to thank our sponsorship from the EPSRC-NIHR HTC Partnership Award.
