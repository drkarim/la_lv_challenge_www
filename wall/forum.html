Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Please join our discussion forum on Google groups for help or starting topics of discussion.

  1. What are the challenge objectives?

    We hope participants will be able to provide an algorithm that is capable of 1) labelling voxels that belong to atrial wall, and 2) compute the atrial wall thickness from the segmentation. However, the second objective remains optional. For more details on the outputs expected, please refer to our forum post on this topic.

  2. When is the submission date for the paper and results?

    The submission date for the paper is 15th June 2016. However, only preliminary results are expected and full analysis can be submitted with the final camera ready version on 1st August.

  3. What data will we be provided for the challenge?

    There are two categories of datasets: MRI and CT. There are 10 datasets in each category. For the MRI datasets, the left atrial endocardial chamber segmentation (i.e. blood pool) is also provided as a separate binary image.

  4. Can we only choose to work on one category, CT or MRI?

    That is absolutely fine if you wish to only segment the atrial wall from either of the two: CT or MRI.

  5. What do we segment in the images provided?

    The challengers are required to obtain a segmentation of the left atrial wall. The left atrium can be seen clearly in each image provided. In the MRI images, the left atrial endocardium or blood-pool segmentation is also provided.
