Algorithms

Five institutions took part in the organized challenge and submitted their algorithms' segmentations. The algorithms were designed at: 1) Department of Imaging Sciences and Biomedical Engineering at King's college London in United Kingdom. 2) Center for Computational Imaging and Simulation Technologies in Biomedicine at Universitat Pompeu Fabra in Barcelona, Spain. 3) Alma IT systems in Barcelona, Spain. 4) Center for intelligence machine at McGill University in Canada. 5) Fraunhofer Institute for Medical Image Computing, Fraunhofer MEVIS in Germany.

Alma IT systems. Support vector machines (SVM)

A number of image processing steps are applied in this algorithm. An Otsu-based threshold performs a crude classification between healthy and scar. This is followed with connected components analysis and support vector machines (SVM). For SVM, several features in the image are considered, for example: area, major minor axes of ellipse-fit, convex-hull area, and Euler number.
