Left ventricle MRI infarct benchmark.

Public datasets for segmenting infarction in myocardium of the left ventricle. The image database consists of 30 multi-center, multi-vendor, and multi-resolution datasets.

Left atrium MRI scar and fibrosis benchmarks.

Public datasets for segmenting fibrosis and scar in myocardium of the left atrium. The image database consists of 60 multi-center, multi-vendor, and multi-resolution datasets.

Standardised evaluation of future algorithms.

The datasets contain ground truth estimations obtained from 3 or more observers using Maximum-Likelihood estimation, evaluation strategies and algorithm ranking.
